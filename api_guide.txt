5J8TC2H58LL009419

1 Unrepaired Recalls
associated with this VIN


Summary
American Honda Motor Co., Inc. (Acura) is recalling certain 2019-2020 model year RDX vehicles. The fuel pump impeller was improperly moulded, resulting in low density impellers. Over time, the low-density impeller can deform and interfere with the fuel pump body, rendering the fuel pump inoperative.

Safety Risk
If the fuel pump module is inoperative, the engine may not start or can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
